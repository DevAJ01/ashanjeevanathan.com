Notes to self

Advice

Action over anxiety.

Complexity is a cost. Simplicity is a superpower.

Build with integrity, even when nobody is watching.

Seek truth, then build. Do not confuse movement with progress.

Be useful. Leave things better. Keep your promises small enough to keep.
