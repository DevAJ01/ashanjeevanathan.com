Philosophy

I want to build a life that compounds quietly: useful work, clear thinking, deep friendships, good books, and the courage to keep simplifying.

My design taste is similar. Start with the reader. Remove anything that performs more than it helps. Make the important paths obvious. Leave enough texture that the page still feels human.
