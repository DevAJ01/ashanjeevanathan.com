Design philosophy

Philosophy

I want this site to feel like a desk, not a stage: useful, legible, and close to the work.

Good design should lower the cost of paying attention. It should make the next thing obvious without making the reader feel managed.

I care about technology, books, architecture, physics, entrepreneurship, and living with more attention. The connective tissue is simple: build things that help people think and act more clearly.
