Find Your Singularity

In an era where every keystroke is archived and every memory is digitized, legacy has shifted from physical artifacts to ethereal bits.

The question is no longer just what we leave behind. It is what we make clear enough for someone else to continue from.

A useful life creates traces that reduce confusion: notes, tools, relationships, decisions, and examples that make the next person’s path a little easier.
