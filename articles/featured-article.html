Find Your Singularity

19th June 2024 2 min read Thoughts
Featured Insight

Created: 19th June 2024 00:53 (Another one of my apple notes where I've written about my thoughts)

ASHAN JEEVANATHAN | FEB 18, 2026

I think a lot of people in todays world lack creativity, but it is not them that is at fault, it is the system that they are placed in, however one can still think and recognise this issue and overcome it.

In todays world, a lot of these young entrepreneurs rush into the idea of a startup and trying to make money as quick and as much as possible. But many of these types of people fail miserably due to one main singularity: Creativity.

For people who want a higher chance of success, they will have need to breathe design and innovate ideas that have yet to exist in todays world, which can ultimately benefit humanity.

Orientate your thinking towards benefiting the lives of the people in this planet, and I assure you, the universe will reward you in many ways, be it with “Net worth” or “publicity” or “satisfaction”.

Creativity, design engineering, is often the issue with many founders and entrepreneurs, their ability to craft a new idea or design a product and be able to communicate that affectively with high class engineers is often what causes the company to collapse.

The least you could do is appear on a wikipedia page to be remembered at least some way in humanity, Humanity will be destroyed before the internet ever does.

Other than that the only remembrance you will get is from your friends and family, but even then, time moves on, Universe keeps expanding etc. and they wont remember you every single day for the rest of their life.

Just like how the Universe started off like a Singularity, that was its big moment which is for ever expanding today.

Seek Your Singularity

So i think people should also seek for their singularity in life and for it to be forever expanding beyond your death, as your ideas and visionaries are still being used and applied.

When you are not alone, your creativity process and your ability to generate new ideas and find solutions to problems often gets disrupted.

I know many software engineers who tend to often randomly find the solution and fix to their code whilst going on about their daily actives alone, whether it just being you picking out an apple from a fridge. In fact Stephen hawking would often go on walks in the country side with his team, in between problems that they are solving. It would be a solitude confinement, just a house, a whiteboard, technological equipment and the nature. Its almost like nature gifts you the solution to your problems.
