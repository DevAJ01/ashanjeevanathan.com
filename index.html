Design philosophy

Make things readable first. Then make them memorable.

I like websites that feel like a person made them: fast, quiet, direct, and full of good trails to follow. The goal is not to impress on the first second. The goal is to reward attention.

Good design removes performance. It makes room for the work, the thinking, and the reader.
